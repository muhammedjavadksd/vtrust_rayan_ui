## **5. University & Accreditation Profiles**

- **University of Mysore:** Established July 27, 1916. Public state university in Karnataka. Autonomous status since 1956. Recognized by UGC.
- **Glocal University:** Established by Glocal University Act 2011 (UP Act 2012). Recognized under Section 22 of UGC Act 1956.
- **BESTIU:** Bharatiya Engineering Science and Technology Innovation University (UGC Approved).
- **NCVRT:** National Council of Vocational and Research Training. Provides government-recognized vocational qualifications.
